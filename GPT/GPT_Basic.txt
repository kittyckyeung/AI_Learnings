
- Now AI. Use natural language / conversation for asking questions.

- Can proceed, analyze, comprehend and generate the nautual language

==================================================================================================
- GPT - Generative Pre-trained Transformer Model
	- Generative - generating human like text
	- Pre-trained - Learn from vast amount of data
	- Transformer - Underlying architecture of the model

- LLMs
	- Neural Network + Attention Mechanism + Transformer Architecture
	- Transformer Architecture
		- Encoder-decoder structure (Input) (Output) 
		- Self-Attention Mechanism (Weight the importance of each work and understand the relatioship)
		- Multi-Head Attention (Allow to play attention to different parts of inputs at the same time)
		- Positional Encoding (To understand the location of the words) 
		- Feed-forward Neural Network (In each layer of Transformer. Process the outputs and capture complex patterns)
